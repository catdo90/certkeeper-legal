Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Introduction

CertKeeper ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address (if provided), industry, role, and professional profile information.
  • Certification Data: Certifications, licenses, vaccinations, CEU credits, work badges, state licenses, employment requirements, and related documents you upload.
  • Device Information: Device name and model (for broken link reporting and support purposes).
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns and preferences to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the CertKeeper service
  • Send you expiration reminders and notifications via push notifications, calendar events, and email
  • Sync your data across devices via Cloud Sync (for both email and Apple Sign-In accounts)
  • Generate compliance reports and export credentials
  • Provide healthcare tools (dosage calculator, unit converters, etc.) - all calculations are performed locally with no data storage
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Monitor and analyze app usage

Data Storage

Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device with encryption. Your certifications, documents, and personal information remain on your device.

Cloud Sync: Your data is synced to secure cloud storage (Firebase/Firestore) to back up and sync across devices. For email accounts, Cloud Sync is always enabled. For Apple Sign-In accounts, you can enable Cloud Sync in Settings. When enabled, your certifications, vaccinations, CEU credits, work badges, state licenses, and profile are stored securely and synced across all devices where you sign in with the same account.

Calendar Sync (Premium Feature): When enabled, CertKeeper creates calendar events in your iOS calendar for certification expirations and employment requirement deadlines. These events are stored in your device's calendar and synced through your iCloud account. We do not access or modify existing calendar events.

Email Reminders (Premium Feature): When enabled, CertKeeper sends email reminders to your registered email address for critical alerts. Your email address is retrieved from your account and used solely for sending reminder notifications.

No Third-Party Sharing (Except AI): We do not sell or trade your personal information. The only exception is when you use AI-powered features; see the AI-Powered Features section below.

AI-Powered Features

CertKeeper offers AI-powered features (resume analysis, career chat, certificate extraction, recommendations). When you use these features:

  • What we send: Resume text, certificate images (when you choose AI extraction), chat messages, and profile data (industry, role, experience) relevant to the feature you are using.
  • Who receives it: Data is sent to OpenAI to generate responses. OpenAI may process and retain data per their Privacy Policy.
  • Your consent: We obtain your explicit consent before sending any data to OpenAI. You must tap "I Agree" in the consent screen before AI features are used. You can choose not to use AI features and still use the app.
  • Storage: We do not store your data on our servers beyond what is needed to provide the feature.

OpenAI provides data protection consistent with industry standards. You can review their practices at OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Healthcare Tools Privacy

CertKeeper includes healthcare-specific tools (Dosage Calculator, Unit Converters, IV Drip Rate Calculator, Shift Checklist). These tools:

  • Perform calculations locally on your device
  • Do not store any patient-identifiable information
  • Are HIPAA-safe - no Protected Health Information (PHI) is collected or stored
  • Do not transmit any data to our servers

All healthcare tools are reference-only and designed to comply with HIPAA regulations.

Apple Sign In

When you sign in with Apple, we only receive your name and email (if you choose to share it). We do not have access to your Apple ID password or other Apple account information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data stored locally is encrypted
  • Cloud Sync uses secure cloud infrastructure (Firebase) with industry-standard security
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) available for additional security
  • Data transmitted for Cloud Sync is encrypted in transit
  • Your documents and certifications are stored securely on your device and in the cloud when sync is enabled

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data via Settings > Account Management > Delete Account
  • Disable Cloud Sync (Apple Sign-In), Calendar Sync, or Email Reminders at any time
  • Export your credentials and data (including Compliance Reports)
  • Request information about data we collect

Account Deletion

You may delete your CertKeeper account at any time through Settings > Account Management > Delete Account. When you delete your account:

  • All your data (certifications, vaccinations, CEU credits, work badges, state licenses, profile, and cloud backups) is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Your local app data is cleared
  • Your authentication account is removed

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at Admin@certkeeper.app and we will delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
