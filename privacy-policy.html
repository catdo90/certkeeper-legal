Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Certkeeper ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address (if provided), industry, role, and professional profile information.
  • Certification Data: Certifications, licenses, vaccinations, CEU credits, work badges, state licenses, and related documents you upload.
  • Device Information: Device name and model (for broken link reporting and support purposes).
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns and preferences to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Certkeeper service
  • Send you expiration reminders and notifications
  • Sync your data across devices via iCloud
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Monitor and analyze app usage

Data Storage

Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device with encryption. Your certifications, documents, and personal information remain on your device unless you enable iCloud Sync.

iCloud Sync: When enabled, your data is synced to Apple's CloudKit service, which uses end-to-end encryption. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is yours alone.

Apple Sign In

When you sign in with Apple, we only receive your name and email (if you choose to share it). We do not have access to your Apple ID password or other Apple account information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data stored locally is encrypted
  • iCloud Sync uses Apple's secure CloudKit service
  • We do not transmit your data to our servers (except for optional broken link reports)
  • Your documents and certifications are stored securely on your device

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Delete your data by clearing all app data in Settings
  • Disable iCloud Sync at any time
  • Export your credentials and data
  • Request information about data we collect

Children's Privacy

Certkeeper is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at Admin@certkeeper.app and we will delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Admin@certkeeper.app
