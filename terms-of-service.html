Description of Service

CertKeeper is a compliance tracking application designed to help licensed professionals manage certifications, licenses, vaccinations, CEU credits, work badges, state licenses, and employment requirements. The App provides:

  • Expiration tracking and reminders
  • Document storage and organization
  • Automatic CEU extraction from certifications (CEU credits extracted from documents are added to your CEU tab)
  • Calendar sync for expiration dates (Premium feature)
  • Email reminders for critical alerts (Premium feature)
  • Compliance report generation (Premium feature)
  • Healthcare tools including dosage calculator, unit converters, and shift checklist (Premium feature)
  • Cloud Sync for data backup across devices (email and Apple Sign-In)
