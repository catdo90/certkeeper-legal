## About CertKeeper

CertKeeper helps licensed professionals manage certifications, licenses, vaccinations, CEU credits, work badges, state licenses, and employment requirements. The app provides intelligent reminders, calendar sync, email alerts, and compliance reports to help users stay compliant and never miss a critical renewal.

## Key Features

- **Compliance Tracking**: Track all professional credentials with expiration dates
- **CEU Auto-Extraction**: When you add a certification, CEU credits are automatically extracted from the document (if present) and added to your CEU tab
- **Smart Reminders**: Multi-channel reminders (push notifications, calendar, email)
- **Calendar Sync**: Automatic calendar events for expiration dates (Premium)
- **Email Reminders**: Critical alerts sent to email as backup (Premium)
- **Compliance Reports**: Professional PDF reports for employer verification (Premium)
- **Healthcare Tools**: Dosage calculator, unit converters, IV drip rate calculator, shift checklist (Premium)
- **Cloud Sync**: Secure cloud backup across devices (email and Apple Sign-In)
- **Biometric Security**: Face ID/Touch ID protection

## Important Disclaimers

**CertKeeper is a reminder and organizational tool. It is NOT a guarantee of compliance.**

- Users remain responsible for verifying all expiration dates and renewal requirements
- Always confirm compliance status with employers and licensing boards
- The app cannot replace official verification processes
